About OwlScript

OwlScript is...

... a programming environment built upon and to resemble JavaScript. It sole purpose to serve as training wheels for programming students of all ages.

Rationale

Young computer science students don't care to learn how to sort an array. Nor do they have any interest studying class inheritance or counting bits in a byte. What they do what is something to show after hours of typing: a picture. OwlCode's focus is on graphics primitives: circles, lines, rectangles, etc. and the programming infrastructure facilitating rendering thereof.

Advantages

JavaScript is chosen for the ubiquity of its interpreter: any modern web browser.

OwlScript is syntactially-forgiving: no need to declare variables; semicolons at the end of statements are not necessary; often-confusing syntactical constructs, such as for loops, are simplified.
